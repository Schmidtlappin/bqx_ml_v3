## üéØ IDX and BQX Transformations

### IDX (Indexed Values)
- **Definition**: Indexed representation of close prices
- **Calculation**: Based on real close prices from forex data
- **Purpose**: Normalized view of price movements

### BQX (Backward Momentum)
- **Definition**: Backward-looking momentum calculation
- **Calculation**: Derived from real price movements
- **Purpose**: Captures momentum patterns in real markets

### Key Point
Both IDX and BQX are **transformations of real market data**, preserving:
- Temporal dependencies
- Market microstructure
- Trading patterns
- Volatility clusters
- Price action characteristics
