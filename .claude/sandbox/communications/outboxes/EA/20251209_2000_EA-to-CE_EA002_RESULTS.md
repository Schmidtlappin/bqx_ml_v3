## Key Findings

### Finding 1: τ=0.80 Meets 85% Target

| Metric | τ=0.70 (Current) | τ=0.80 (Proposed) | Change |
|--------|------------------|-------------------|--------|
| Accuracy | 82.52% | 86.23% | **+3.71%** |
| Coverage | 78.84% | 65.83% | -13.01% |
| Signals | 52,442 | 43,789 | -8,653 |

**Trade-off**: Gain 3.7% accuracy by sacrificing 13% coverage.

### Finding 2: Coverage Still Above Target Range

- Target coverage: 30-50%
- τ=0.80 coverage: 65.83%
- τ=0.85 coverage: 59.17%

Even at τ=0.85, coverage remains above the 50% upper target. This suggests the model produces confident predictions frequently, which is positive.

### Finding 3: Diminishing Returns Above τ=0.80

| Threshold Step | Accuracy Gain | Coverage Loss |
|----------------|---------------|---------------|
| 0.70 → 0.75 | +1.77% | -6.39% |
| 0.75 → 0.80 | +1.94% | -6.62% |
| 0.80 → 0.85 | +2.11% | -6.66% |

Gains are consistent but coverage drops more steeply at higher thresholds.
